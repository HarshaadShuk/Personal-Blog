About Me

Hello there! I'm Harshaad, a student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), where I'm deeply immersed in the world of science. My studies fuel my curiosity, pushing me to constantly ask questions and seek understanding about the world around us.

This blog is my personal corner of the internet to document my journey. I write about concepts that fascinate me, the ups and downs of university life, and other hobbies that keep me busy, like coding and astrophotography. My goal is to share my passion in an accessible and engaging way.

When I'm not buried in textbooks, you can find me exploring new coding projects or trying to capture the beauty of the cosmos with my camera. Thanks for stopping by!
